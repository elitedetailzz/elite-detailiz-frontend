Services

These are the services we offer

Exterior & Interior

Our interior deep cleaning refreshes and sanitises your cabin, while exterior detailing safely removes dirt and grime, restoring gloss, protection, and a near-new finish overall.

Ceramic Coating

Elevate your car's protection and gloss with our ceramic coating application, creating a hydrophobic and durable barrier.

Paint Correction

Our expert service eliminates swirls, scratches, and oxidation while restoring gloss and clarity. Using precision techniques and premium service, we tailor the process to your paint's condition giving your car a showroom finish.

Scratch Repair

Our scratch repair service minimises light to moderate scratches and paint defects, restoring a smooth, uniform finish.

Headlight Restoration

Our headlight restoration service removes oxidation and yellowing, restoring clarity and brightness through precision sanding and polishing, improving visibility, safety, appearance, and delivering a durable like-new finish.
